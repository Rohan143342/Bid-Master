Getting Started

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to start bidding!

You'll need to provide:
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Payment method

Bidding

To place a bid:
  1. Find an item you want to bid on
  2. Click "Place Bid"
  3. Enter your maximum bid amount
  4. Confirm your bid

Auto-bidding allows you to set a maximum bid amount. Our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to that amount when others place bids.

Selling

To list an item:
  1. Click "List Item" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in item details and upload photos
  3. Set your starting price and duration
  4. Review and publish your listing
