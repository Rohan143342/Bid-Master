How BidMaster Auctions Work

Learn how to buy and sell items on our platform with confidence. Our simple process makes online auctions easy and secure.

For Buyers For Sellers

The Auction Process

Browse Auctions

Explore our wide range of items across various categories. Use filters to find exactly what you're looking for.

Place Your Bid

Enter your maximum bid amount. Our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to your limit.

Win the Auction

If you have the highest bid when the auction ends, you win! You'll receive a notification immediately.

Complete Payment

Pay securely through our platform using your preferred payment method.

Receive Your Item

The seller will ship your item directly to you. Track your delivery through your account.

For Buyers

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free BidMaster account. Verify your email and add your shipping information.

Add Payment Methods

Securely add your credit card, PayPal, or other payment methods to your account.

Save Favorites

Add items to your watchlist to track auctions you're interested in.

Set Bid Alerts

Receive notifications when you're outbid or when auctions you're watching are ending soon.

Buyer Tips

  • Research similar items to determine a fair price before bidding
  • Read the item description and view all photos carefully
  • Check the seller's rating and feedback from previous buyers
  • Consider shipping costs when determining your maximum bid
  • Use the "Ask a Question" feature if you need more information

For Sellers

List Your Items

Create detailed listings with high-quality photos and accurate descriptions.

Set Starting Price

Choose a competitive starting price and auction duration (3, 5, or 7 days).

Monitor Your Auctions

Track bids and respond to buyer questions through your seller dashboard.

Receive Payment

Once the auction ends, the buyer pays through our secure platform.

Seller Tips

  • Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
  • Be honest about item condition and any defects
  • Set a reasonable starting price to attract more bidders
  • Ship items promptly and provide tracking information
  • Respond quickly to buyer messages to build trust

Fees and Policies

Buyer Fees

0%

Buyers pay only the winning bid amount plus shipping. No additional fees.

Seller Fees

5%

Sellers pay a 5% commission on the final sale price, with a minimum fee of $1.

Payment Processing

2.9% + $0.30

Standard payment processing fees apply to all transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

To place a bid, simply click the "Place Bid" button on any auction item. Enter your maximum bid amount, and our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to that limit. You'll be notified if someone outbids you.

If you win an auction, you'll receive an email notification immediately. You'll have 48 hours to complete payment through our secure platform. Once payment is confirmed, the seller will ship your item directly to you.

To list an item, click "Sell" in the top navigation and select "Create Auction." Upload photos, write a detailed description, set your starting price and auction duration, and publish your listing. Your item will appear in our marketplace immediately.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted platform.

If you don't receive your item within the expected delivery timeframe, first contact the seller through our messaging system. If the issue isn't resolved, our customer support team will help you file a claim under our Buyer Protection policy.
